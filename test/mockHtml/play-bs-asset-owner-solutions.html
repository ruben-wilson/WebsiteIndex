Enterprise Data

Solutions
Value for Clients
Fund Data
Asset owners need access to fund constituent data to understand what external asset managers are investing in. Bloomberg has an extensive data set for mutual funds and ETF holdings data across fixed income and equity managers. Asset owners also need to see descriptive data of the fund such as AUM, fees and benchmarks.
Sustainability Data
ESG & Sustainability Data - Asset owners may need to assess ESG and sustainability data on their portfolios to ensure they match their principles on responsible investing and comply with regulations like SFDR and the EU Taxonomy. Some create exclusion lists and policy mandates that they give to external asset managers to follow. Datasets of interest can include ESG, ESG Scores, Carbon Estimates, Sustainable Debt, and SFDR & EU Taxonomy.
Liquidity Data
Asset owners will want to understand the liquidity risk of the portfolios managed by their external asset managers. Bloomberg's LQA solution allows them to look at this data in their internal system across multiple securities and asset types.
Pricing
Good quality pricing information for the funds and underlying securities is important for asset owners to understand the performance of their portfolios. Bloomberg has several high-quality independent pricing sources including BVAL, CBBT and BGN.
