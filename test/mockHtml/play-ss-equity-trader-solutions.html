Enterprise Data

Solutions
Value for Clients
Price & Volume Information
Traders need access to real-time quality pricing and volume information so that they can quickly spot opportunities in the market and understand liquidity. B-PIPE provides traders this real-time quality information.
Corporate Actions
Traders need to be alerted to anything that may change their positions or affect the price of the securities they are market making on. Getting quality timeline information about corporate actions and stock splits is critical for a trader to be able to price securities accurately. Bloomberg offers both a real-time corporate actions feed (CANS) and an end-of-day file including pre-market activity.
Earnings Calendar
Traders need to be aware of when a company is reporting earnings and sales calls as there tends to be market volatility after an announcement, especially if there is a surprise. Bloomberg offers both a real-time events feed (EVTS) and an end of day file containing all upcoming events.
Estimates
Understanding the markets expectation of a companies performance is critical to understand the fair price of a security. If a company misses expectations at earnings, traders need to be prepared for market volatility.
Dividend Forecasts
Important for traders to understand expected exercise dates for securities and sentiment around dividends as this can affect investor demand. Likewise certain trading roles such as delta one or sec lending who may lend out a security or go short need to know expected dividend information as they will need to factor this into their premiums given the dividend payment is due to be paid to the beneficial owner.
Sanctions
Sanctions information can be fed into pre-trade compliance checks to prevent traders trading sanctioned securities.
News
Bloombergs Events Driven Feed (EDF) spans a number of use-cases including news headlines, sentiment and story bodies. Sophisticated algo traders may want to feed this information into their pricing algo to achieve a better execution or more accurate pricing.
