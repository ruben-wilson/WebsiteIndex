Enterprise Data: Credit

Solutions
Value for Clients
Pricing Data
Understand the price of an instrument using multiple pricing sources such as BVAL, CBBT and MSG1.
  • BVAL: For accurate pricing and benchmark comparison with third parties. Especially important for illiquid securities. Selling point : Wide coverage.
  • CBBT: Shows composite pricing from dealers and is considered timely. Same use-case as BVAL, albeit for more live vs evaluated data.
  • MSG1: Prices sent to the firm by sell-side trader runz. These are prices that are being quoted directly to the dealing desk.
Holdings Data
Sales want to know which clients hold which securities. This helps them identify who would be interested in which securities.
Corporate Structure/ Capital Structure
By combining Bloomberg's corporate structure and capital structure, clients are able to identify all the debt that has been issued by an issuer.
Sustainable Debt
Understand which bonds are green and flag use of proceeds.
New Issue Feed
Traders need to have the new securities set up and mastered in their internal system as quickly as possible so tha they can position to clients.
Default Risk
Understand the default risk of a company and its securities.
