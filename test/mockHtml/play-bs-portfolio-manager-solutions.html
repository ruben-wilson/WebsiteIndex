Create a list of equities that match a set of criteria, based on terminal or custom data (eg. custom ESG ratings). Integration with Terminal solutions (eg. SRCH

Fixed Income Search
Screen for bond issuances, loan deals and structured instruments to support idea generation. Use natural language to specify relevant search criteria, eg. allowing PM's to generate a list of investment grade corporate issuances in a certain industry with a single entry. Integration with other terminal solutions (eg. FIW<GO> and GC<GO>) allows the users to dissect the instruments based on criteria like the interest rate sensitivity that they offer and fit them onto a custom curve.
Market Monitoring, Portfolio Construction
EQBT
Equity Backtesting
Construct and backtest model portfolio strategies. Clients can choose something as simple as modelling an equal allocation to each security in a pre-defined market or implementing a custom factor-based strategy. Offers immediate access to key summary performance statistics for the model portfolio, allowing them to evaluate its profitability and risk profile. Further integration with PORT allows PM's to get a deeper dive into the model's characteristics and run performance and risk attribution analysis relative to their fund or a target benchmark index.
Portfolio Construction
PORT
Portfolio Opimization
A flexible tool that allows PM's to generate a list of optimal trades required to achieve a target allocation with desired portfolio characteristics. This may be perhaps required to rebalance a passive fund's realignment with benchmark, or to realign the fund's allocation to ensure compliance with regulatory or mandate-driven requirements. Can also be used for construction and backtesting of a portfolio strategy, based on risk factor bets or conventional analytics.

Enterprise Data

Solutions
Value for Clients
Company Fundamentals
Portfolio managers need to be able to analyze the fundamentals of a company,and compare performance to estimates and guidance. This is,to identify attractive opportunities that align with a fund's mandate. Clients are also interested in understanding how revenue is broken down within a company across the different markets it operates in. Potential datasets include Company Fundamentals, Estimates, Guidance, and Revenue Segmentation.
Corporate Structure / Debt Distribution
By combining Bloomberg's corporate structure and capital structure data, clients are able to identify and assess all the debt that has been issued by an issuer.
New Issues
PM's need to have new issues quickly set up in their OMS and PMS so that their traders can execute on those deals and the data can flow into their portfolio. This can be done in a timely fashion with a solution like the New Issue Feed.
Sustainability Data
Bloomberg has a wide range of sustainability products across ESG, such as reported data, Sustainable Debt, Climate Emissions, Regulations amongst many others. This is especially important for portfolios that have a green mandate such as SFDR Article 8 and 9 funds.
Pricing Data
Access to quality pricing data is integral to valuing a fund correctly. BVAL provides a consistent and high quality methodology for analysing the price of FI securities within the portfolio and can be used to calculate the NAV of a portfolio.
Corporate Actions
PM's need to be aware of any corporate action that may affect the securities in the portfolio such as M&A activity, dividends and rights issues. Bloomberg's corporate actions offering allows clients to get this data at the end of the day and also as a premarket file. Bloomberg also has a real-time corporate actions (CANS) service on B-PIPE. More systematic funds can use this to quickly adjust positions if they need to.
Sanctions
PM's need to have access to Sanctions data so that they can avoid adding sanctioned securities to the portfolio. Bloomberg's Fund Exposure solution also allows fund-of-fund managers to understand if a fund under consideration holds any sanctioned securities.
Liquidity Information
It is important for a PM to get a robust estimate of the liquidity risk that their portfolio carries. Bloomberg's LQA allows the client to access our liquidity models in the clients portfolio on a security basis, and LQAP allows clients to view this data across a portfolio.
