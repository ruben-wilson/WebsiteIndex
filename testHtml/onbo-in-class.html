Cloud Essentials

Cloud Essentials

ACloudGuru.com is the premier online learning cloud computing platform which FSKG makes available to Sales reps. This Bloomberg-tailored ACloudGuru curriculum includes Labs, where you can experiment by creating AWS Cloud resources such as instances, databases, permission policies like a real web architect. The Cloud Essentials for Bloomberg Sales requires you to take a basics course, technical course, lab work and to watch a Bloomberg Cloud Computing video series. BQL Online Bootcamp

BQL Online Bootcamp

The Bloomberg Query Language (BQL) is a groundbreaking API that will help users get direct answers to some of their most pressing challenges. The BQL Online Bootcamp program covers basic and intermediate knowledge of the BQL syntax and queries. This course will teach you how to master BQL and position it like a pro. ... read more

Requirements: Complete 6 Hours of course modules to receive a certificate of completion.

BQL Certification

BQL Certification

This BQL Certification provides an introduction to BQL and BQuant and how to articulate the value proposition to clients. You'll learn how different player types can use BQL in their workflow, how to demo basic BQL queries and the BQL Builder in Excel, and how to contrast BQL and BQuant as two distinct products that work well together. The course provides practice interactions and case studies to help you develop skills to spot opportunities to position BQL and BQuant to clients. ... read more

Requirements: Complete 3 hours and 30 mins of course modules and pass an exam.

Environmental Social Governance Certification

Environmental Social Governance Certification

The Bloomberg ESG Certificate is a self-paced, interactive e-learning course that shows learners how to implement ESG strategies and prepare ESG reports for regulators through the lens of a fictitious buy-side asset management firm. You'll follow practitioners at the firm as they launch new ESG products to meet client demand and implement ESG strategies into their investment process. ... read more

Foundational Level 2

Foundational Level 2

Instructor-Led Course

This instructor-led course focuses on the different asset classes (FX/ECO, Commodities, Equities, Fixed Income, and PORT). It gives a mini-specialist course on each topic. It is designed for new hires in Analytics. If there is space, seats can be made available for Sales if the Sales Team Leaders and Analytics Program Managers approve. To join a Foundational Level 2 asset class course you need to commit to attending in person for 19 full days of the course and take all exams for that asset class.

    Supporting videos for FL2:
  • Introduction to Fixed Income 40min
  • Introduction to FX & Economics 13min
  • Introduction to Commodities 17min

Specialist classes provide a comprehensive look at a particular asset class as well as the suite of tools used by its most prominent players. The instructor-led courses are designed for new hires in Analytics and cover all content needed to understand an asset class from a Bloomberg perspective at the level of a "specialist". They are full-length courses that have at least one exam but may have more. If there is space, seats can be made available for Sales if the Sales Team Leaders and Analytics Program Managers approve.

    FSKG Program Managers:
  • AMER: Alex Vendrell & Florian Dejean
  • EMEA: Merel Turksema
  • APAC: Cheryl Wong
