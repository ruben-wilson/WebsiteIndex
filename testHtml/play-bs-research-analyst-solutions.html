Enterprise Data: Rates

Solutions
Value for Clients
Pricing Data
Analysts need access to quality rates pricing information across the sovereign and swaps market to feed into models and applications as well as downstream systems. This includes sources such as CBBT and BMRK for sovereign bonds, and BGN and CMPN for swaps.
Volatility
Analysts look at implied volatility to see pricing expectations in the market. Bloomberg calculates interest rate volatilities, across the futures and OTC markets, on a large number of currencies globally under the BVOL umbrella. B-PIPE licensing allows for this data to be fed into models and applications.
Curves
The shape of the yield curve and future expectations of rates are important for analysts to understand the current and future health of the economy. Our data allows clients to pull market rates and derived data (zero rates, discount factors, and forward rates) for standard dates and customized tenors.
Macro & Economic Data
Feed data such as GDP, non-farm payroll and inflation numbers into research models as they impact recommendations for securities. In addition clients may be interested in understanding implied rates from WIRP which is available via DL.
Individual Economic Estimates
See each analyst's contributed estimates for economic releases such as GDP and non-farm payrolls. History available.
Liquidity
Primarily used for risk to understand market liquidity of securities and market impact.
Default Risk
Understand the default risk of a sovereign (SRSK).
