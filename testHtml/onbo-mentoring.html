Mentoring

Our program allows you to receive personalised coaching and shadowing support from your mentor (your Team Leader or senior Relationship Manager) and helps you uncover additional resources for further development.

Define your mentoring goals

Define...
Work with your Team Leader to define what areas you want to be mentored on.
1min

Find a mentor

Find...
Work with your Team Leader to identify who can be your best mentor.
1min

Book an introduction meeting

Book...
Start with an introduction meeting to set objectives, talk through the logistics, etc. Consider inviting your Team Leader to this initial conversation.
1min

Book a meeting

Book a meeting with your mentor at least 4 times over 3 months.

Select two skills

Share the skills you selected with your mentor and discuss how you want to improve. The aim is to work through all 6 topics over the 3 months.

Sales Culture
Networking
Sales Operations
Sales Skills
Industry and Product Knowledge
Engaging Clients and Sales Strategies

Ask questions

Ask questions and share your thoughts and concerns, this is an open forum for you to self-reflect. This will help you assess your current skill level. Take notes to track your progress.

Share your notes

Share your skills assessment notes with your mentor and review any areas you found challenging or would like support in to personally develop.

Discuss with your mentor

Your mentor will provide coaching and advice throughout the discussion.

Drive results

Drive...
Aim to walk away from each session with a SMART goal.
2min

Shadowing

Book a client meeting

Book a client meeting and ask your mentor to shadow you. They should fill out the feedback form at the back of this booklet to provide you with constructive feedback and guidance.

Arrange a shadow

Arrange...
Arrange to shadow a Salesperson to see what techniques and style they use when facing clients. Aim to shadow at least two reps in your first three months. Note down your feedback in this booklet.
1min
