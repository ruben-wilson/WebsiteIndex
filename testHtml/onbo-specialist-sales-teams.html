Jeff Leckstein

Global Head of Buy-Side Enterprise Sales

PORT & Risk sales put the industry’s most powerful Portfolio & Risk Analytics Solution (PORT) and Bloomberg's Multi-Asset Risk System (MARS) at the fingertips of global investment professionals.

PORT Enterprise helps the front and middle office achieve their objectives with an architecture that provides unmatched transparency into portfolio performance, characteristics, risk and more.

MARS is powered by Bloomberg’s world-class pricing library, market data and mortgage cash flow engine. MARS enables front-office, risk and collateral professionals to analyze their trading and investment portfolios, manage and mitigate their exposure and ready themselves for any turn of events. The team is responsible for driving the sales and engagement process by identifying client needs and demonstrating how PORT Enterprise and MARS can be used to streamline portfolio management, risk and reporting.

Bloomberg's Buy-Side Order Management System (AIM) delivers global, multi-asset solutions for portfolio management, trading, compliance, and operations for Buy-Side firms.

Our goal is to offer the most scalable asset management technology in the market - one that allows AIM clients to grow assets on existing investments and to quickly expand into new asset classes, markets, or investment styles without disruption.

The team is responsible for engaging with existing and prospective AIM clients to deliver value by streamlining workflows across the complete investment life-cycle.

BSKT is Bloomberg’s primary market solution offering ETF issuers, market makers, authorized participants, distributors, custodians and investors the world’s first system for negotiation, ticketing and routing ETF creation and redemption orders.

Our goal is to partner with industry participants to streamline the workflow and communication for ETF creation and redemption. The team is responsible for engaging with market participants and building a community who leverage BSKT as a central tool in their ETF workflow.

RMS helps our customers build and manage tools that allow buy side analysts to employ an efficient research process helping to make money for the firm through profitable investment decisions.

The goal is to is empower our clients by surfacing relevant research content into a centralized view.

The team works closely with our clients to drive adoption of our enterprise research tools as well as uncovering new enterprise opportunities.

Electronic Trading

Electronification is changing the trading landscape as we know it, but our clients' goals are still the same — simply put, they need tools that fit the way they trade. Traders and portfolio managers at the world’s leading financial institutions are partnering with Bloomberg’s electronic trading team to access a comprehensive global network of deep liquidity for multi-asset class trading. Before, during and after the trade, access comprehensive end-to-end solutions across the trading life cycle, bringing together pricing, analytics, liquidity, automation, and execution optimized by straight through processing so they can connect more successfully to the global trading markets — all in one place.

Hank Anderson

Global Head of Electronic Trading Sales

Bloomberg’s fixed income trading platform and complete execution management solution provide liquidity, trading functionality and straight-through processing for all fixed income securities, derivatives and futures.

Our full suite of products include:

FIT

Bloomberg’s fixed income trading platform (FIT) links all available fixed income electronic trading products into one portal and provides multi-dealer, executable composite pricing, so you can stage, monitor, trade and allocate trades through a single function on the Bloomberg Terminal. You can also trade with FIT from your TSOX blotter.

BOLT

Bloomberg BOLT is list trading platform that allows you to access a deep pool of liquidity for investment grade, high yield, emerging markets (EM), credit default swaps (CDS), interest rate swaps (IRS) and municipal bonds.

BMTF

Bloomberg multilateral trading facility is a robust trading platform for trading cash bonds, IRS, CDS, Repos, ETFs, listed options and FX derivatives. Bloomberg has examined and built upon its existing end-to-end trading workflows to offer an efficient trading platform and ensure a seamless transition into the MiFID II regulatory environment.

Bloomberg’s Listed Trading Solutions supply the optimal blend of data, integration, connectivity and ease of use to assist clients in achieving their best execution practices. We provide our clients with a global solution for listed trading markets, delivering a full trade life cycle workflow solution for listed equities, futures and options, including ETFs, derivatives and ADRs.

Our full suite of products includes:

IOI Liquidity Discovery

Filter relevant IOI content and set alerts on select names by integrating content from our global network of 3,700+ broker destinations with Bloomberg lists and market data.

BTCA

Use pre-trade tools like TCA to identify and address outlying orders that need attention. Then rely on BTCA for post-trade analytics and reports.

Electronic Trading

For the Buy Side, Bloomberg’s Execution Management Solution provides access to algos, dark pools, cash desks and ECNs. For futures, destinations include algos and direct market access (DMA), and for options, an integrated solution includes RFQ. For the Sell Side, we offer an advanced multi-asset execution tool with access to client order flow, analytics on client orders and algo distribution.

Flexible Trade Automation

Bloomberg offers Buy-Side traders the ability to apply rules to order flow via Rule Builder (RBLD) and the Broker Wheel, assisting you in achieving your best execution policy goals in a productive, consistent and timely manner.

BSKT is Bloomberg’s primary market solution offering ETF issuers, market makers, authorized participants, distributors, custodians and investors the world’s first system for negotiation, ticketing and routing ETF creation and redemption orders.

FXGO provides streaming or RFQ for spot, outrights, swaps, NDFs, deposits and options in any currency pair and tenor, and is included with a Bloomberg Terminal subscription.

BTBU

Bloomberg Tradebook United States is an electronic trading negotiation system for transacting in financial instruments.

BTBS

Bloomberg Tradebook Singapore is global trade negotiation system that provides liquidity across numerous assets.

SEF

Bloomberg Securities Execution Facility provides all participants access to multiple execution styles and liquidity across numerous asset classes including rates, credit, currency and commodity derivatives. Bloomberg SEF is registered with the Commodity Futures Trading Commission (CFTC), with participants actively trading Permitted Transactions in CDS, IRS, FX and commodity derivatives.

BTCA

Having effective transaction cost analysis and surveillance capabilities is essential — whether a trader is looking to get decision support for strategy selection, a portfolio manager is trying to understand trading-cost impact on fund performance or a compliance officer is mandated to manage regulatory risk.

BTCA offers a solution that is simple-to-use and delivers actionable results. BTCA offers a solution that is simple-to-use and delivers actionable results.

Bloomberg’s Trading Connectivity services clients’ cross-asset connectivity needs for trade, post-trade and liquidity discovery. Our product offering includes analytics for liquidity and price discovery to support trading decisions.

Our goal is to provide our clients information and control of their execution process regardless of the order and execution management system they use.

BNEF

BNEF Sales is a global team of sales specialists for Bloomberg's strategic energy transition research solution BNEF. BNEF covers global commodity markets and disruptive technologies driving the transition to a low-carbon economy. BNEF's Sales Reps work with the rest of Bloomberg Sales to shdowcase BNEF for Buy-Side clients, particularly for thematic research and idea generation. The team also showcases BNEF for Sell-Side clients for energy industry expertise and especially project finance, and for corporate clients' strategy teams looking to adapt to the energy transition.

Sales Relationship Map

Strategic Goals 

BNEF's goal is to enable corporate strategy, finance and policy professionals navigate change and generate opportunities in this global transition.

Products/Functions the Role Covers

BloombergNEF (BNEF) is Bloomberg's strategic energy transition research solution, covering the disruptive technologies that drive the transition to a low-carbon economy.

BNEF's content team is comprised of nearly 300 global experts across commodities, clean power, advanced transport, heavy industry, innovative materials, agriculture and land use, and other cross-cutting clean technologies.

BNEF research assesses pathways for the power, transport, industry, buildings and agriculture sectors to adapt to the energy transition. This provides visibility on medium- to long-term strategy and forecasts, policy, finance and economics, sustainability and consumers. Combined with Bloomberg Terminal Data, BNEF's proprietary cost data, in-depth primary research and forecasts provide a unique insight into markets in transition across the globe.

    BNEF offers a range of products including:
  • Primary research and forecasts: From quick commentary and short-term views to deep analysis and long-term forecasts, and covering all areas of BNEF expertise.
  • Data, models and tools: A rich suite of analytical tools and proprietary models, supported by Bloomberg’s unparalleled data capabilities.
  • Analyst access: Specialists ready to answer your research questions on any sector, market or technology.
  • Briefings and live webinars: Presentations focused on cross-cutting industry and regional topics.
