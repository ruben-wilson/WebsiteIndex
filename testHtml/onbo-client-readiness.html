Client Readiness

Congratulations! You are at the final stage of your onboarding journey. Now is the time to apply what you have learned on your development path and evaluate client readiness through a role play.

1:30min

Identify a Client

Together with your Team Leader, identify a client to engage. This could be an existing Bloomberg client or a new prospect. The selected client should reflect a typical client type that you will engage with in your role. During the role-play exercise a sales specialist will assume the role of this client. Your team leader will observe the role-play and provide feedback.

Gather Information

  • Firm Level Intelligence

    Overview of the firm - what they do and why

  • User Intelligence

    Information of the person you are meeting

  • News & Industry Trends

    Key tools for news and industry trends that are affecting your client

Create Your Outline/Strategy
  • Goal for this engagement
  • GCOM methodologies
  • Questions to ask
  • Anticipated objections
  • Plan for approaching the conversation

Conduct Roleplay and Gain Feedback

You will conduct the roleplay at a time arranged by your Team Leader.

A specialist will join to assume the role of your selected client. Your Team Leader will observe and facilitate the feedback discussion.

Once the roleplay is done, you will reflect and discuss feedback to gain insight and agree on next steps for your continued development.
