Company Fundamentals
Understand the health of your company and compare it to your peers. Bloomberg can provide data on fundamental data from income statements to balance sheets and also provide detailed revenue breakdown information in our Revenue Segmentation files
Security Performance Data
IROs are very interested in how their company is performing vs their peers. They would want to be able to chart this and look at certain performance metrics. Bloomberg has the abiliity to provide this data into their internal systems.
Security Ownership
Investor relations departments will be interested in understanding who owns their securities or their peers securities as this will intimate who is most interested in investing in the company.
Sustainability Data
IROs are interested in understanding how many SFDR Article 8 and 9 funds hold their securities. They therefore keep a close eye on their own sustainability credentials and Bloomberg has solutions such as ESG as reported, Carbon Emissions, SFDR and EU Taxonomy data amongst many others. IR would look at this data on themselves and their peer group.
Sanctions/Supply Chain
Clients are very interested in ensuring that they have robust supply chains and also being able to spot issues such as if there are any sanctioned companies somewhere in their supply chain.
Estimates Data
IROs are interested in understanding the market concensus on their performance and peer group. Blomberg can provide BEST estimates that they can ingest into their internal systems.
